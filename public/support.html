Frequently Asked Questions

What is RealEngine?
RealEngine is a professional AI image generation application that uses advanced FLUX models to create high-quality images from text descriptions. It includes features like face matching, transparent backgrounds, and advanced editing capabilities.
How do I generate an image?
Simply enter a description of what you want to create in the text box and click the send button. The AI will generate a high-quality image based on your prompt in just a few seconds.
What are the different generation modes?

How do I use Face Match (PuLID)?
Select the "Face Match" mode, upload a reference image containing the face you want to maintain, then describe the scene you want to create. The AI will generate images that preserve the facial features from your reference image.
What is the Vector mode for?
Vector mode (LayerDiffuse) generates images with transparent backgrounds, perfect for logos, icons, and design elements that need to be placed over other content.
Can I use custom LoRAs?
Yes! You can add personal LoRAs in the settings panel. Enter your LoRA name, AIR ID, and optional trigger words to use your custom trained models.
How much does it cost?
RealEngine uses accelerated processing to provide 30-70% cost savings compared to standard generation. Pricing varies by feature, with most generations being very affordable.
Is my data secure?
Yes, your prompts and generated images are processed securely with enterprise-grade privacy protection. We don't store your generated images or personal prompts.
